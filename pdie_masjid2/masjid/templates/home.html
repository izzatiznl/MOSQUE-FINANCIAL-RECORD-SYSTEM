About Us

Masjid Qaryah Kampung Rinching Hulu is a central place of worship, learning, and community service for the residents of Kampung Rinching Hulu. The mosque not only serves as a venue for daily prayers and religious classes, but also plays a vital role in uniting the local community through various social and spiritual initiatives.

The mosque regularly organizes events such as tazkirah sessions, charity drives, gotong-royong activities, and youth programs aimed at strengthening faith and fostering cooperation among villagers. It is managed by a dedicated committee with the support of volunteers who ensure the smooth operation of mosque affairs.

With a growing number of programs and responsibilities, the mosque embraces technology to improve administrative efficiency and financial accountability,helping the community thrive in both spiritual and social aspects.
